Tech specs

The detail behind the hold.

Cold-rated, impact-resistant components engineered for arena conditions. The P3G anchor mechanism withstands repeated game-speed contact, daily install/removal cycles and a full season of facility use without fatigue or loosening.
Designed for standard regulation goal frames used in minor-hockey, junior, college and professional rinks. Works across the common net profiles found on North American ice sheets — confirm your frame in the dealer enquiry for facility orders.
One-person, roughly 60-second setup. Anchors seat firmly into the ice and pull clean at the end of session — no tools, no drilling, no surface damage. The net holds position through pushes, post-integration and contact, then releases when you want it to.
Backed by a manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Facility and dealer orders include direct support and replacement-part access. Reach the team anytime at hello@finngoalie.shop.
