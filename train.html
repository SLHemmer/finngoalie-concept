Before you book

Good to know.

Pick by your goalie's age and current level — every program lists its age band (e.g. U9–U13) and whether it's foundational, weekly, or elite/AAA. Unsure? Start with a single private session and we'll recommend the right track in person. Or build a free training plan and we'll match a program to it.
Every camp runs the full Finngoalie system: Xtracker vision blocks, live-read drills, edgework and recovery, and small-group coaching. Multi-day camps include daily on-ice plus off-ice vision and movement work. The exact daily schedule and what to bring is sent on enrollment.
A single session ($49.99+) is the no-commitment way to try the work. A package ($325+) bundles multiple sessions at better value — the sweet spot for most goalies. A season pass ($899.99+) is weekly 1-on-1 ice all year with the same coach for one continuous development arc.
On-ice programs run at partner rinks across the Halifax / Bedford area; synthetic sessions run at our dedicated off-ice training surface. Each program's confirmation includes the exact location, parking and arrival time. Travelling in? Message us — we'll help you plan.
Life happens. Cancel a single session up to 24 hours ahead for a full credit. Camps are transferable and refundable up to two weeks before the start date. Reach out any time and we'll make it right — we'd rather keep a goalie than a deposit.
