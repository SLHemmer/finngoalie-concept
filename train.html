For parents

Built to be trusted.

You're not buying ice time — you're buying development. Here's what every program guarantees.

Small ratios

Goalie-specific groups, capped low. Your kid gets real reps and real eyes on every drill — never lost in a crowd of skaters.

Video review

Sessions are filmed and broken down so progress is visible — not guessed. Goalies see exactly what changed, week to week.

Good to know

Questions parents ask.

Full goalie gear, a water bottle and a stick. For synthetic sessions, regular running shoes are fine off-ice. We supply pucks, nets, Finnpegs anchoring and all training aids — including the Xtracker on vision-focused days. Arrive 15 minutes early to dress.
Camps and packages can be rescheduled up to 7 days before the start date, subject to availability. Single sessions can be moved up to 48 hours out. Refunds are available before a program begins, less a small admin fee; once a multi-week block has started it's credited, not refunded. Injury? Send us a note — we'll work with you.
Every program lists its age band (U9–U20) and level — foundational, all-levels, high-performance, or AAA/elite. Foundational and all-levels suit recreational and most minor-hockey goalies; high-performance, Prospects and AAA streams are for competitive goalies. Unsure? Book a single session first and we'll recommend the right track.
Programs run across arenas in the Halifax / Bedford area of Nova Scotia, with synthetic-ice sessions at our dedicated off-ice training space. Each program's exact rink and primetime ice slot are confirmed on your booking and emailed before the first day.
