Why the Club exists

Most goalies train alone. They shouldn't have to.

A camp is a week. A private session is an hour. But the position is won in the months between — the reps nobody sees, the film nobody reviews. I built the Club so every goalie gets a coach in their corner all year, not just on a rink rental. Same obsession we bring to a Pro Week — now it shows up every month, for the price of a stick tape order.
